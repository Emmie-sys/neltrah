About NeltraH Technologies

We are committed to driving technological innovation across the country

Our Journey

Founded in 2025 in Gulu, NeltraH Technologies began with a mission to bring world-class tech solutions to Uganda. Over the past times, we've grown alot; with the aim of becoming a leader in East African technology innovation.

Our Mission

To empower Ugandan businesses and communities through transformative technology solutions that drive economic growth and improve quality of life across all regions of the Country.

Our Vision

To establish Uganda as Africa's next technology innovation hub by 2030 through education, infrastructure development, and world-class digital solutions.

Our Services: Engineered for Digital Excellence

NeltraH Technologies Ltd. delivers end-to-end IT solutions designed to drive innovation and maximize operational efficiency across diverse sectors. Our comprehensive service portfolio includes:

Computer Training and Certification

We provide practical, beginner-friendly and advanced computer training for individuals, students, and corporate teams.

Our curriculum includes basic digital literacy, Microsoft Office tools, typing skills, email usage, coding basics (Python, HTML, CSS), and more. Each course ends with a certificate of completion to boost your credentials.

  • Builds digital confidence and job-readiness
  • Flexible schedules and affordable fees
  • Suitable for both beginners and professionals

Graphic Design

We create stunning visual content to promote brands, products, and events.

Our graphic design services include logo design, flyers, posters, social media graphics, brochures, banners, business cards, and product packaging. We use industry-standard tools like Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, and Canva.

  • Boosts brand visibility and professionalism
  • Custom designs tailored to your audience
  • Fast delivery and print-ready formats

Website Design and Development

We build modern, responsive, and user-friendly websites for individuals, businesses, and organizations.

Whether it’s a portfolio, company profile, e-commerce store, or school system, we develop secure and visually appealing websites using technologies like HTML, CSS, PHP, WordPress, and MySQL.

  • Strong online presence for your brand
  • Mobile-friendly and SEO-optimized
  • Hosting and maintenance support available

Cloud Computing and Cybersecurity Solutions

With us, you can migrate to the cloud and secure your digital infrastructure.

Our services include cloud storage setup, online backups, email solutions (like Google Workspace or Outlook), data protection, firewall setup, and basic cybersecurity training.

  • Secure and scalable IT environment
  • Reduces data loss and downtime
  • Enables remote work and collaboration

Computer Repair and Maintenance

We diagnose and fix hardware and software issues in desktops, laptops, and accessories.

Services include virus removal, software installation, system upgrade, formatting, screen and keyboard replacement, and general cleaning plus many more.

  • Prolongs device lifespan
  • Saves costs on new purchases
  • Quick, affordable, and reliable service

AI and Digital Data Analytics

We offer beginner-level AI solutions and data analytics services

for small businesses, including chatbot setup, Excel analytics, Google Analytics, and basic machine learning insights using Python or Power BI. We help you turn raw data into clear, actionable insights.

  • Supports smart business decisions
  • Automates repetitive tasks
  • Helps track customer behavior and performance

ICT Consultancy Services

We offer expert guidance for individuals, schools, businesses, and institutions seeking to adopt or improve their ICT systems.

From choosing the right tech solutions to project management and system integration, we help you achieve efficient digital transformation.

  • Informed tech investment decisions
  • Custom solutions based on your needs
  • Ongoing technical support
